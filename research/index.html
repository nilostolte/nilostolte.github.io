Research
In this section, a list of previous selected research work in the area of computer graphics, published in scientific journals, books and international conferences is shown. This work is mainly in ray-tracing, implicit surfaces, interval arithmetic, robust voxelization, 3D rendering, point rendering, octree as well as parallel processing.
Recent research and development work in 2D vector graphics for Graphics User Interfaces is briefly explained here and detailed in the next sections.
